# User Guide

This guide explains how to navigate and use the AI-Native Textbook effectively.

## Navigation

The textbook presents content in a sequential, step-by-step format:

1. **Chapters**: Major topics are organized into chapters
2. **Steps**: Each chapter contains multiple learning steps
3. **Progression**: You must complete each step before advancing

## Tracking Progress

- Progress bars show your advancement through each chapter
- The dashboard provides an overview of your overall learning progress
- Completed steps are marked with a checkmark
- In-progress steps are highlighted

## Personalizing Your Learning

Adjust your learning experience based on your background:

1. Visit your **Profile** page to update your background information
2. Select your experience level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
3. The system will adapt content delivery based on your preferences
4. Use the **Learning Path Selector** to choose different approaches to content

## Completing Steps

Each learning step may include:
- Reading materials
- Interactive exercises
- Quizzes or assessments
- Practical applications

Mark steps as complete when you've understood the content. The system validates completion before allowing progression to the next step.
