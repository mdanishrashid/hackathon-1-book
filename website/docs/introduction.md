# Introduction to AI-Native Textbook

Welcome to the AI-Native Textbook for Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics! This interactive learning platform provides a step-by-step approach to mastering complex concepts in AI and robotics.

## What You'll Learn

The AI-Native Textbook guides you through complex topics using:

- **Sequential Learning Steps**: Follow a structured path through each concept
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your advancement through chapters and modules
- **Personalized Learning**: Content adapts to your background knowledge
- **Interactive Content**: Engage with concepts through hands-on exercises

## How It Works

1. **Select a Chapter**: Choose from available learning modules
2. **Follow the Sequence**: Complete each step in order to build understanding
3. **Track Progress**: Visualize your advancement through the material
4. **Customize**: Adjust learning paths based on your experience level

## Getting Started

Browse the available chapters from the navigation menu to begin your learning journey. Each chapter breaks complex topics into digestible, sequential steps designed to build your knowledge progressively.
